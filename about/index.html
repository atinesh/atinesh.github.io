About

Hello, my name is Atinesh Singh, I am a Machine Learning Engineer based in India. I have a Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Computer Science. Over the years, I’ve developed a deep interest in areas like machine learning, natural language processing (NLP), large language models (LLMs), evolutionary algorithms, and knowledge graphs.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading books, writing blogs to share knowledge with the community, and contributing to open-source projects. I also like to watch TV series, and getting lost in immersive video games. Some of my all-time favorite video games include The Witcher 3 and Elden Ring.

If you’re interested in collaborating, discussing ideas, or just want to connect, feel free to reach out to me at atinesh.s@gmail.com. You can also explore some of my work on GitHub.
